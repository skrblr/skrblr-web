Default

/home - takes you back to the home you set
/sethome - sets your home location
/spawn - takes you back to spawn
/call [name] - sends request to teleport to [name]
/msg [name], /m [name] - sends private message to [name]
/who - displays online players
/motd - displays motd
/rules - displays rules
/compass, /whereami - displays location information

Builder

[everything default can do, and the following]
/shock - shocks yourself, if you need to suicide
allows placing signs on chests and doors to lock them

Trusted

[everything builders can do, and the following]
/warp [warpname] - teleports you to the given warp. you need to know the name.

Donator

[everything trusted can do, and the following]
/warps del [name] - delete warp with specified name
/warps list - list all warps
/item, /i [id] [quantity] - spawns items into your inventory with the specified id
/clear - clears your inventory
/setwarp [warpname] - sets a warp at your location
/tp [name] - teleports you to the given person
/put - puts you at the spot you're looking at
/return - puts you at your previous location
can use worldguard to protect regions (google how to do this)

Moderator

[everything trusted can do, and the following]
/warps list - lists all warps
/warps del [name] - deletes warp [name]
/setwarp [name] - sets a warp at your current location
/put [name] - teleports [name] to the place you're looking at
/pex user [name] group set [default, builder, trusted] - sets player [name] to the given group
/md [sheep, chicken] - turns you into the given creature
/hawkeye - lets you use hawkeye, google this one more
/honeypot - lets you set blocks as honeypots to catch griefers, google this one more
/broadcast - broadcasts a message as ItBePuddles
/clear - clears your inventory
/ban [name] [reason] - bans [name]
/kick [name] [reason] - kicks [name]
/mute [name] - mutes [name]
/tp [name] - teleports you to [name]
can use worldguard to protect regions

Giver

[everything moderators and donators can do, and the following]
/slap [name] - slaps [name]
can break signs on locked chests and doors
