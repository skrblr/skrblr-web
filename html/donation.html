Perks of donating

You can donate any amount of money to get a blue name.

For $20 dollars, you receive the first donator rank, which allows you to spawn items

Once you achieve the first donator rank, you can buy creative ($15) or worldedit ($15) or both!

Only moderators are eligible to purchase VoxelSniper ($15).


Please note:

Put your Minecraft name in the comments section of the donation form! There is no other way for me to tell who donated.

Donating does not give you a license to grief.

You must be at least "trusted" rank to donate. If you have a red or white name, and you choose to donate, I will not rank you up until you obtain trusted status.
